Paracetemol

an analgesic and antipyretic drug that is used to temporarily relieve mild-to-moderate pain and fever. The recommended paracetamol dosing for adults and children 12 years and over is 500 to 1000mg every four to six hours as necessary, with a maximum of 4000mg in any 24 hour period.
